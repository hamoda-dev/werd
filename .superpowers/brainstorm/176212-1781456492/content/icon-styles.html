Delicate 1.5px strokes, geometric and minimal. Stays outline in both states — only the colour shifts to gold. Quietest, most “minimal & geometric”.

filled → filled
Bold filled silhouettes in both states — muted when inactive, gold when active. Strongest presence, the most “app-store icon” look.
